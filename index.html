Educator | Researcher

Hey, I 'm Md. Rafiqul Islam, from Dhaka, Bangladesh. Lecturer in the Department of CSE at  Institute of Science, Trade, and Technology . This institute is under the National University curriculum. In these courses, I have given lectures on: Structured Programming Language (CSE-510201), Structured Programming Language Lab (CSE-510202), Computer Architecture (CSE-520205), Data and Telecommunications (CSE-530203), Data and Telecommunications Lab (CSE-530204), Computer Networking (CSE-530210), Computer Networking L ab (CSE- 530211 ), Network and Information Security (CSE-540209), Network and Information Security La b (CSE- 540210 ), Digital Systems Design (CSE-510208), Digital Systems Lab (CSE-510209), Web Engineering (CSE-421), Web Engineering Lab (CSE-422), and E-Commerce (CSE-427).

Adjunct lecturer in the Department of CSE at Central University of Science and Technology . In these courses, I have given lectures on: Computer Architecture (CSI-01 126), Peripheral and Interface (CSI-01 127), Peripheral and Interface Lab (CSI-01 227), and Software Entrepreneurship (CSI-01 124).

Faculty of Computer Science at Masco School Kanchan. The Cambridge Assessment of International Education (CAIE) is implemented at the school. I work with students in the Mid and Senior level divisions. Specifically, I am administering the IGCSE O and A levels. I am the club manager for the Programming and Robotics Club at our school. In particular, I teach robotics like Arduino and programming languages like Python, Java, and C++ to our pupils.

I was researching for one year at the university time of last year and one paper was published at an international conference SUSCOM-2019 : International Conference on Sustainable Computing in Science, Technology &Management . Another paper was completed waiting for a good international conference to submit it.
